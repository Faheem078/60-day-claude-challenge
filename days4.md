Step 7 — Networking Strategy
Comment before you post. For the first 30 days, spend 20 minutes daily leaving thoughtful, value-adding comments on posts by AI engineers, tech founders, and developers. This gets your name in front of their audiences before you have your own.
Connect with intention. Send 5 targeted connection requests per day — AI professionals, recruiters at tech companies, fellow student builders, LinkedIn creators in tech. Always add a short personal note.
Engage your own community. Reply to every comment on your posts within the first hour. The algorithm rewards early engagement velocity.
Find your 10 core creators. Identify 10 LinkedIn voices in AI and Web Dev whose audience matches who you want to reach. Show up consistently in their comments with genuine insight — not "great post!" but real additions to the conversation.
Cross-promote between platforms. If you have GitHub projects, link them. If you build on Replit or deploy on Vercel, share the live links. Proof of work is the best networking tool.
